Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories. We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as result of comsuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view legal page for more information

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pround of suger
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperatue for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!.
